Just One More: Helping Leeds Eat More Veg Every Day

Just One More is a local campaign to encourage people across Leeds to eat Just One More portion of vegetables every day as a snack or in their meals.

If you want to be inspired to eat ‘Just One More’ in your meals, check out the Recipe hub for lots of tasty recipes.

All resources to support the campaign can be downloaded below.

Did you know
  • 1 in 3 adults consume 5 or more fruit and veg per day
  • 2 in 3 Leeds children / young people consume at least 3 portions of fruit and vegetables per day
  • Salad, tomatoes, carrots and peas remain the most commonly eaten veg in the UK
  • Veg is consumed most often in home cooked meals such as curries and casseroles
  • Increasing vegetable intake can have a significant positive impact on the planet
