Become Foodwise.

From sharing what we have to sharing what we know, we’re building stronger communities through the power of food.

Whether you’re a Leeds resident, a food business owner or part of a local organisation, discover easy ways to ‘become FoodWise’ and take action to help  create a healthy, sustainable and fair food system for our city that builds social, economic and environmental justice.
